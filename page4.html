YRASEGUI V PAL

G.R. No. 168081
October 17, 2008
ARMANDO G. YRASUEGUI, petitioners,
vs.
PHILIPPINE AIRLINES, INC., respondents.

FACTS 

Petitioner was a former international flight steward of PAL, herein respondent. Petitionerwas dismissed because of his failure to adhere to the weight standards of the airline company.Petitioner claims that he was illegally dismissed.


ISSUE

Was the dismissal valid?

HELD

SC upheld the legality of dismissal. Separation pay, however, should be awarded in favorof the employee as an act of social justice or based on equity. This is so because his dismissalis not for serious misconduct. Neither is it reflective of his moral character.
